52 /// Tiles `op` by `sizes` permuting the loops according to `permutation`
53 /// and sets the attribute `kLinalgTransformMarker` to `linalgMarker`.
54 /// The permutation is expressed as a list of integers that specify
55 /// the new ordering of the loop nest. The length of `permutation`
56 /// must be equal to the length of `tileSizes`.
57 /// E.g. the permutation `(i,j,k) -> (j,k,i)` will be expressed with
58 /// `permutation = [1,2,0]`. All values in `permutation` must be
59 /// integers, in the range 0..`tileSizes.size()` without duplications
60 /// (i.e. `[1,1,2]` is an invalid permutation). An empty list
61 /// states for the identity permutation.
62 LogicalResult tileLinalgOpAndSetMarker(PatternRewriter &rewriter, Operation *op,
63  ArrayRef<int64_t> sizes,
64  StringRef linalgMarker,
65  ArrayRef<unsigned> permutation);
