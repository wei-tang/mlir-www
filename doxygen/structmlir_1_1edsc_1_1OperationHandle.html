Detailed Description

An OperationHandle can be used in lieu of ValueHandle to capture the operation in cases when one does not care about, or cannot extract, a unique Value from the operation.

This can be used for capturing zero result operations as well as multi-result operations that are not supported by ValueHandle. We do not distinguish further between zero and multi-result operations at this time.
