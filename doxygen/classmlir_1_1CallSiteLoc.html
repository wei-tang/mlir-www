Detailed Description

Represents a location as call site.

"callee" is the concrete location (Unknown/NameLocation/FileLineColLoc/OpaqueLoc) and "caller" points to the caller's location (another CallLocation or a concrete location). Multiple CallSiteLocs can be chained to form a call stack.
