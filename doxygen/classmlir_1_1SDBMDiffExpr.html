Detailed Description

SDBM difference expression.

LHS is a direct expression, i.e. it may be a sum of a term and a constant. RHS is a term expression. Thus the expression (t1 - t2 + C) with term expressions t1,t2 is represented as diff(sum(t1, C), t2) and it is possible to extract the constant factor without negating it.
